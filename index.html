West Hollywood presents the annual Halloween Carnaval

October 31, 2018 from 6:00 pm to 10:30 pm

Live music & DJs, killer costumes, dancing, Local eats and fun! Santa Monica Blvd between North Doheny Drive and La Cienega. 6:00 pm to 10:30 pm

Things to Know

  • Yes, the Halloween Carnaval is free

    No tickets are required, but pay attention to some of the guidelines on this page.

  • Kids should probably sit this one out

    We're sure your children are great, but this might be a good night to get a babysitter.

  • Parking can be... scary

    While the city has parking structures available, we recommend using designated rideshare dropoff and pickup zones.

  • OMFG! There will be plenty of music

    For your listening pleasure, enjoy multiple music stages along the boulevard blasting live sets and Dj tracks.

  • No, you can't drink alcohol on the street

    Plenty of places along the street will be open for business, here's a list of restaurants, bars, and clubs.

  • Don't be a jerk

    To keep the festival fun for everyone, don't start start trouble. Oh, and please carry items in a clear plastic bag. More Halloween safety information.
